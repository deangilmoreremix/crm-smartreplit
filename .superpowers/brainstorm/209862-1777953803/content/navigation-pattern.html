How should the Playground be organized?

8-10 stations need structure. Pick a navigation pattern.

A

Tabbed Playground

One section with horizontal tab navigation. Each tab reveals a self-contained interactive demo. Tabs: Contacts → Enrichment → Scoring → AI Chat → Pipeline → Analytics → Email → SMS → Video → Forecast.

Best for: focused exploration, easy to scan

B

Scroll Tour with Sticky Nav

Left sidebar with station list stays sticky as you scroll through full-width station sections. Each section is a full interactive demo. Progress indicator shows where you are.

Best for: immersive storytelling, cinematic feel

C

Station Cards + Expand

Grid of preview cards (3x3 or 2x4) showing a screenshot/icon of each station. Click any card to expand it into a full interactive demo in a modal or side panel. Quick overview, dive in on demand.

Best for: visual variety, non-linear exploration
