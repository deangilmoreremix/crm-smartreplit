SmartCRM Showcase Site Strategy

Three approaches to turn your app into a million-dollar showcase with interactive demos

A

Interactive Feature Playground

Each feature gets a live, sandboxed mini-demo on the landing page. Visitors click "Try It" and interact with real (or realistic mock) data — contact enrichment, AI scoring, pipeline drag-and-drop — without signing up. Heavy on wow-factor, requires the most build effort.

B

Cinematic Product Story

A scroll-driven narrative experience that walks visitors through a day in the life of a SmartCRM user. Auto-playing screen recordings, animated data visualizations, and contextual feature callouts. Less interactive, more emotional and polished. Easier to build, harder to differentiate.

Hybrid: Guided Tour + Live Sandbox

Start with a cinematic hero and product story, then transition into an interactive "Playground" section where visitors can actually use key features with demo data. Best of both worlds — emotional hook + hands-on proof. Recommended.
