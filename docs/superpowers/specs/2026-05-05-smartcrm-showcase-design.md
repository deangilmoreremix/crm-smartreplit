# SmartCRM Landing Page Design Specification
**Date:** 2026-05-05
**Purpose:** Conversion-optimized showcase landing page built on existing landing page infrastructure

---

## 1. Concept & Vision

A high-converting SaaS landing page that tells the story of SmartCRM's AI-powered CRM platform through bold messaging, social proof, and interactive feature demos. The page follows a narrative arc: **problem → solution → proof → demo → offer**. Visitors experience the product's power before being asked to buy.

The design is bold, confident, and energetic — matching the brand personality in PRODUCT.md. Every section has one job: move the visitor closer to purchase.

---

## 2. Page Structure (13 Sections)

### Section 1: Hero (Above the fold)
**Job:** Capture attention, establish value, drive first CTA click

**Elements:**
- **Headline:** Bold, benefit-driven (e.g., "The AI-Powered CRM That Closes Deals 35% Faster")
- **Subheadline:** 1-2 sentence value prop
- **Product screenshot/video:** Auto-playing dashboard demo (muted, ~30s loop)
- **Primary CTA:** "Get Instant Access — $X/month"
- **Secondary CTA:** "See It In Action" (scrolls to tour)
- **Trust badges:** 5000+ users, 99.9% uptime, SOC 2 compliant

**Layout:** Full-width, dark gradient background with floating dashboard screenshot

---

### Section 2: Social Proof Bar
**Job:** Build credibility immediately after hero

**Elements:**
- **Auto-scrolling client logos** (6-8 logos)
- **Stat counters:** "5000+ Users" | "35% Sales Growth" | "9.5 Hours Saved/Week"
- **One-sentence testimonial** with avatar

**Layout:** Horizontal strip, subtle background, logos grayscale→color on hover

---

### Section 3: Problem Agitation
**Job:** Validate the visitor's pain before presenting solution

**Content mirrors InstantlyClaw's "The Problem" section:
- CRM complexity is overwhelming sales teams
- Manual data entry eats hours every week
- Disconnected tools create chaos, not productivity
- Generic CRMs don't understand modern sales workflows

**Format:** 4 pain points with icons, emotional copy, minimal visuals

---

### Section 4: The Tour — Interactive Feature Showcase
**Job:** Let visitors EXPERIENCE the product before buying

**Structure:**
- Sticky left sidebar with 9 station links
- Progress indicator ("Station 3 of 9")
- Full-width station content on right

**9 Tour Stations:**
1. **Contacts Explorer** — Searchable contact list with filters
2. **AI Enrichment Demo** — Click "Enrich" → animation → populated fields
3. **AI Lead Scoring** — Score breakdown with rationale panel
4. **AI Assistant Chat** — Interactive chat with suggested prompts
5. **Pipeline Kanban** — Draggable deals across stages
6. **Analytics Dashboard** — KPI cards + interactive charts
7. **Email Composer** — Rich text with AI assist suggestions
8. **SMS Hub** — Message thread with quick templates
9. **Video Call Widget** — Live preview with call controls

**Micro-CTAs:** "Get This Feature" button appears after each station

---

**Features:**

## 3. Visual Design

### Color Palette
| Token | Value | Usage |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Primary | #3b82f6 (blue-500) | CTAs, links, accents |
| Primary Dark | #2563eb (blue-600) | CTA hover |
| Background | #0f172a (slate-900) | Hero, dark sections |
| Surface | #1e293b (slate-800) | Cards, panels |
| Text | #f8fafc (slate-50) | On dark backgrounds |
| Text Muted | #94a3b8 (slate-400) | Secondary text |
| AI Accent | #8b5cf6 (violet-500) | AI feature highlights |
| Success | #10b981 (emerald-500) | Positive stats, checks |

### Typography
- **Headlines:** System font stack, bold weight, 2.5-3.5rem
- **Subheadlines:** 1.25-1.5rem, medium weight
- **Body:** 1rem, regular weight, 1.6 line-height
- **Buttons:** 0.875rem, semibold, uppercase tracking

### Spacing System
- **Section padding:** 5rem top/bottom (desktop), 3rem (mobile)
- **Content max-width:** 1280px
- **Card padding:** 1.5rem
- **Gap between elements:** 1-2rem

### Motion
- **Scroll-triggered fade-in:** 300ms ease-out, staggered 100ms
- **Hover states:** 150ms transitions on buttons/cards
- **Stat counters:** Count-up animation on scroll-into-view
- **Testimonial carousel:** Auto-rotate every 5s, pause on hover

### Component Styles
- **Cards:** Rounded-xl, subtle border, hover:shadow-lg
- **Buttons:** Rounded-lg, primary gradient, hover:scale(1.02)
- **Testimonial cards:** Photo left, quote right, star rating
- **Feature icons:** 48x48px, rounded-lg, colored background

## 4. Tour Station Specifications

### Station Wrapper Component
```tsx
<TourStation>
  <StationSidebar />
  <StationContent>
    <StationTitle />
    <InteractiveDemo />
    <StationDescription />
  </StationContent>
</TourStation>
```

### Station 1: Contacts Explorer
- **Real component:** Use `ModuleFederationContacts` with demo data
- **Features shown:** Search, filters, contact cards, detail panel
- **Demo data:** 10 pre-populated realistic contacts

### Station 2: AI Contact Enrichment
- **Mock:** Contact detail view with "Enrich" button
- **Animation:** 2-second loading → fields populate with data
- **Show:** Company, role, social profiles, enrichment score

### Station 3: AI Lead Scoring
- **Real component:** Score display with breakdown
- **Show:** Engagement score, firmographic score, behavioral score
- **Rationale panel:** "Why this score" explanations

### Station 4: AI Assistant Chat
- **Mock chat interface:** Pre-loaded conversation
- **Suggested prompts:** "Find contacts without emails", "Score my top 20 leads"
- **Sample responses:** AI-formatted results

### Station 5: Pipeline Kanban
- **Real component:** `PipelineModal` or Kanban board
- **Demo data:** 8-10 deals across 4 stages (Lead, Qualified, Proposal, Closed)
- **Interaction:** Drag a deal between stages (visual feedback only)

### Station 6: Analytics Dashboard
- **Real components:** KPI cards + charts from dashboard
- **Data:** 30-day view, animated chart on load
- **KPIs shown:** Revenue, conversion rate, pipeline value

### Station 7: Email Composer
- **Mock:** Rich text email with AI assist sidebar
- **Features:** Template selection, AI tone adjustment, send preview

### Station 8: SMS Hub
- **Mock:** Message thread UI
- **Quick templates:** "Follow up", "Meeting reminder", "Deal update"
- **Character count:** Visible SMS length indicator

### Station 9: Video Call Widget
- **Real component:** `VideoCallPreviewWidget`
- **Show:** Call controls, preview window, connection status

## 6. Content Guidelines

### Tone
- Confident, not arrogant
- Specific, not vague ("35% more deals" not "significantly better")
- Benefit-focused, not feature-focused
- Bold personality, energetic language

### Avoid
- Generic SaaS phrases ("seamless", "robust", "cutting-edge")
- Passive voice
- Negative comparisons to competitors
- Over-using exclamation points

### Copy Examples
**Hero:** "The AI-Powered CRM That Closes Deals 35% Faster"
**Subhead:** "SmartCRM Enriches Contacts, Scores Leads, and Automates Outreach — So You Can Focus on Selling"
**CTA:** "Get Instant Access — No Setup Required"

## 7. Responsive Behavior

### Breakpoints
- **Mobile (<640px):** Single column, hamburger nav, full-width cards
- **Tablet (640-1024px):** 2-column grids, collapsible sidebar
- **Desktop (>1024px):** Full sticky nav, 3-column grids, hover states

### Tour Navigation
- Mobile: Horizontal scroll tabs at top, swipe between stations
- Tablet/Desktop: Sticky left sidebar

### Images
- Dashboard screenshots: Rounded-lg, subtle shadow
- Logos: Grayscale → color on hover
- Testimonial photos: Circular, 48px
